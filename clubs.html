Reading Club

Our Reading Club is a welcoming space for those who love exploring the world through books. Whether you're into fiction, non-fiction, self-help, or poetry, our goal is to foster a love for reading, improve language skills, and create engaging discussions around great literature.

What We Do

  • Weekly book selections (based on interest and level)
  • Group discussions and reflections
  • Vocabulary highlights and reading strategies
  • Guest readers and author spotlights

Benefits

  • Improve your vocabulary and comprehension
  • Discover new genres and authors
  • Boost your writing and speaking skills
  • Make new friends through shared interests

Conversation Club

The Conversation Club is the perfect space to practice speaking in a fun, relaxed, and supportive environment. It's ideal for learners of all levels who want to boost their confidence and fluency in real-world conversations.

What We Do

  • Weekly themed topics and guided conversations
  • Ice-breaker games and role-playing
  • Pronunciation and fluency practice
  • Guest speakers and cultural exchanges

Benefits

  • Practice real-life English in a friendly setting
  • Learn idioms, slang, and natural expressions
  • Get real-time feedback from peers and instructors
  • Build confidence and social connections

Useful Resources to Practice

  • BBC Learning English
  • Elllo.org
  • ESL Lab
  • Voice of America – Learning English
  • YouGlish
